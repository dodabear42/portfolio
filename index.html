About Me

I am passionate about web accessibility.
I live in Michigan with my family of humans and animals. I've loved tech since I first talked my parents into allowing me to adopt an old used PC a friend was getting rid of. I was intrigued by PC components and how to make them work together.

As I continue to learn and grow as a developer, I build on my ability to create websites that are accessible to all. I want my sites to be created with people in mind who have various abilities because some barriers should not be there in the first place. When I’m not making digital things, I’m building more tangible creations like scarves and furniture.
